HOW TO PLAY


  • All 4 players must be logged in to start the game.
  • Press "NEW GAME" button to start the game.
  • The goal is to recreate the reference image shown to you on the screen on your canvas.
  • Each player can only paint with their chosen color.
  • You have 2 minutes to recreate paint the reference image displayed as accurate as possible.
  • The game ends when the time has run out OR when all players have pressed the "FINISHED" button
  • Your goal is to get as a high accuracy percentage as possible.
